Warhammer - Building, painting and battling with miniatures

Miniature wargaming

Warhammer, or at its core, miniature wargaming, is a multi-faceted hobby and is enjoyed in different ways by different people, but most people in the hobby engage at least somewhat in all of the activities.

Firstly, it gets us in touch with our creative side through the assembling and painting process.

Did you know?

Some model painters spend over a 100 hours painting and modifying a single model for competitions. The most famous competition is the Golden Demon

More reasons to play Warhammer!

Secondly, there is the cognitive element. We get to engage our brains through tactics and strategy that need to be executed to the best of our abilities when we battle on the tabletop.

Lastly, but in my opinion most importantly - It gets us away from our screens to socialize with friends and other enjoyers of the hobby at the local game store. This is a fantastic way to meet new friends, share fun moments together, and just have a good time.

The hobby isn't without its drawbacks, as any person that is involved in this hobby will tell you - it can get very expensive!

Some notes on spending your money wisely

As discussed in Psychology Today (2025), people spend money on hobbies for different reasons.

It's important to understand the reasons you are spending your money on a hobby, and make sure that you are satisfied with your own reason(s).

That being said, as long as you watch your budget, feel free to have fun!
